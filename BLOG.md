# Automate Your Product Growth with the New UserPilot n8n Node

We're excited to announce the release of our latest community node for n8n: the UserPilot integration! As automation specialists at Velocity BPA, we understand the challenges product teams face when trying to connect their growth tools with the rest of their tech stack.

## The Problem We're Solving

UserPilot is an incredible platform for driving product adoption, collecting user feedback through NPS surveys, creating onboarding flows, and segmenting users. However, manually syncing this data with your CRM, analytics tools, or support systems creates bottlenecks and limits your ability to act on insights in real-time.

That's where automation comes in.

## What Can You Do With This Node?

The n8n-nodes-userpilot integration enables you to seamlessly connect UserPilot with over 400+ other applications in your workflow. Here are just a few possibilities:

- **Sync NPS responses** directly to your CRM or data warehouse for deeper analysis
- **Trigger personalized flows** based on user behavior captured in other tools
- **Update user segments** automatically when specific conditions are met across your stack
- **Create checklist completions** when users accomplish goals in your product
- **Send alerts** to Slack or Teams when important user milestones occur

The node provides comprehensive access to UserPilot's API, allowing you to read and write data programmatically within your n8n workflows.

## Getting Started

Installing the UserPilot node is straightforward. Simply run:


npm install n8n-nodes-userpilot


Once installed, restart your n8n instance, and you'll find UserPilot available in your node library. Connect your UserPilot account using your API credentials, and you're ready to start automating!
